Learning Experiences Reflection


Yale Young Global Scholars Programme (Politics, Law, Economics)


The YYGS programme is the most diverse programme that I took part in.
I got to meet and interact with people from all over the world. It was
a very unique academic programme where I go to learn about a lot of
different perspectives.

There were many discussions predicated on topics like the architecture
of captivity, the monopolies of the past and present, cross-cultural
communication and many more. Participating in the discussions and also
listening to others share their experiences and views was great. It
broadened my perspectives and made me understand many concepts in ways
that I could never have imagined.

Also, the teachers had very interesting presentations and activities
prepared for all participants. Presentations which provided a great
deal of relevant information on various topics which are of importance
to the world were presented to us and the breakout discussions after
the presentations ensured that the learnings from the presentations
remained in the learnersâ€™ memory.

The overall programme allowed me to see multiple processes, mechanism
and systems in a new light and expanded my knowledge at the same time.
It also inspired me to explore more and to feed the fire of my curiosity.
