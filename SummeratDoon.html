Learning Experiences Reflection


Summer at Doon Leadership Programme


The Summer at Doon Leadership Programme was the first international
programme that I took part in. Interacting with people from different
backgrounds and cultures was amazing. I learned that there are no barriers
of communication set in place between people of different cultures as I
was abel to communicate with all the participants and we were able to
exchange many ideas while going through the programme.

I also learned how to work together as a team while going through obstacle
course, creating a short play, playing as a team in sports and also helping
each other learn in the content oriented sessions. Moreover, learning to
develop collaborative skills made be both a better leader and follower.

We also looked at different styles of leading in order to understand that
leading is different for different people. Leadership has a few guaranteed
qualities but the style of leading can change depending on both the leader
and also the team that she/he is leading.
